Canadian OpenStack User Group

The Canadian OpenStack Users Group, or CanStack, is passionate about working together across Canada to help make the OpenStack project a success.

Supported in part by Cybera and the OpenStack Foundation, we help members by organizing meetups and providing remote video and audio conferencing capabilities.
